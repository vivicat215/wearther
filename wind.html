We all hate when the weather isn't just beautiful and perfect, so we always want to make sure that we are well equipped with our favorite hairstyles for windy days that'll keep it in place and gorgeous. The goal is to keep it simple and manageable. No one wants their lovely hairstyle to get ruined by a million flyaways! So, take a look through below chart that'll make things much easier for the unpredictable weather.

Speed(mph) Name Condition
Less than 1 Calm Smoke rises vertically
1-11 Light breeze Wind felt on face, flags extended, leaves move
12-24 Moderate breeze Dust and small branches move, small trees begin to sway
25-38 Strong breeze Large branches move, wires whistle, umbrellas are difficult to control, whole trees in motion, inconvenience in walking
39-54 Gale/ Strong gale Difficult to walk against wind. Twigs and small branches blown off trees, minor structural damage may occur (shingles blown off roofs).
